About our future tech

Tech Educa is the global online learning platform that offers anyone, anywhere access to online courses and degrees from world-class universities and companies.

Tech Educa was founded by Aminu Abdulrasheed and is team Ng in 2023 with a vision of providing life-transforming learning experiences to learners around the world. Today, Coursera is a global online learning platform that offers anyone, anywhere, access to online courses and degrees from leading universities and companies. Coursera received B Corp certification in February 2021, which means that we have a legal duty not only to our shareholders, but to also make a positive impact on society more broadly, as we continue our efforts to reduce barriers to world-class education for all.
